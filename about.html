OUR HISTORY

When we first started as a local small batch brewer in London, Ontario, we sought to make simple great tasting beer. Over the years and through many test we have refined our brewing process and created rigorous quality control we achieved our goal creating our signature PAMPERS Beer.

Recently we announced the launch of out 2 new beer flavours, Baby-Pale Ale, and Baby Imperial. Both of our new brews follow the same rigorous testing you've come to expect from us and we know that you will enjoy them as mush as our normal Baby-Dry Lager.
